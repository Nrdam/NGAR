Guide

How to View in AR

Follow these simple steps to experience products in augmented reality

1. Open on Your Phone

Open this page on your mobile device, or scan the QR code with your phone's camera to launch the 3D viewer directly.

2. Tap the AR Icon

Look for the AR icon in the bottom-right corner of the 3D viewer and tap it to launch the augmented reality experience.

3. Point at a Flat Surface

Move your phone to scan a flat surface like a floor or table. The model will appear in your real-world space — drag to reposition.

AR works on iOS 12+ (Safari) and Android 8+ (Chrome)
